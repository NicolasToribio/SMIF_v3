GAME Forum

April 3-4

  • Hosted by Quinnipiac University in New York City, the Global Asset Management Education (GAME) Forum connects students with top investment professionals through panels, workshops, and live portfolio competitions.
  • It's a rare opportunity to network directly with Wall Street leaders and experience the fast-paced world of global asset management firsthand.

CFA Research Competition

Prospective Date

  • Teams research and analyze a publicly traded company, then present a detailed equity report and valuation to a panel of industry experts.
  • The competition mirrors real-world investment analysis, sharpening your financial modeling, presentation, and teamwork skills at a professional level.

College Fed Challenge

October 3

  • The Fed Challenge simulates a real Federal Open Market Committee meeting, where students analyze economic conditions and propose monetary policy recommendations.
  • It's perfect for those interested in economics, finance, and policymaking, offering an insider's view into how central banking decisions are made.
